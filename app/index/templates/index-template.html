The Teaching Kit

The teaching kit offers recipe cards for implementing project-based learning in the classroom. Through project-based learning, students get to deeply engage with course concepts, practice design thinking, and collaboratively solve problems.

What is a reciep?

Each recipe provides a unique angle on facilitating a project-based university course. They are designed to aid the teaching process, from lecturing design thinking concepts to grading project deliverables.
