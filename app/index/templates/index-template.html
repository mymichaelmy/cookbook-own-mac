The Teaching Cookbook

The teaching cookbook is a kit offering recipe cards for implementing design-led learning in the classroom. Through design-led learning, students get to deeply engage with course concepts, practice design thinking, and collaboratively solve problems.

What is a recipe?

Each recipe provides a unique angle on facilitating a design-led university course. They are designed to aid the teaching process, from lecturing design thinking concepts to grading project deliverables.
